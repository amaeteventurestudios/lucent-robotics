The Technology

PRECISION.
SCALE.
CONTROL.

Lucent Assembler Systems are teams of modular hexapod robots that coordinate autonomously to build structures of any scale. Each unit is a stacked Stewart platform, two plates connected by six linear actuators, delivering full six-degree-of-freedom movement with accuracy that existing industrial robots cannot match at both short and long reach simultaneously.

Applications

WHERE
WE BUILD.

Space validates the platform. Earth scales the business. Lucent Robotics targets every environment where humans cannot safely, efficiently, or economically build at the precision and scale required.

01
Solar Array Construction

Autonomous assembly and expansion of orbital solar infrastructure. Precision placement of structural members in locations that prohibit human access or sustained teleoperation.

02
Habitat Module Assembly

Connection and structural joining of habitat modules in orbit. Error detection and correction in real time, without waiting for human intervention.

03
Antenna + Telescope Structures

High-precision support structure construction for orbital science equipment. Long-reach capability with accuracy that existing robotic arms cannot match.

04
Refueling + Logistics Infrastructure

Framework assembly for orbital refueling stations, logistics carriers, and external platform integration. Charged per structure, per mission, per deployment.

01
Housing + Modular Construction

Structural frame assembly, roof truss installation, and prefab panel joining at speed and scale. Partners with modular homebuilders working in standardized component environments.

02
Bridge Construction + Maintenance

Long-reach precision placement of structural members in locations humans cannot safely reach. Over 40,000 structurally deficient US bridges represent an immediate federal procurement opportunity.

03
Rail Infrastructure

Track laying, tunnel lining, elevated span assembly, and emergency repair deployment. Aligned with DoT and federal infrastructure funding for rail modernization and expansion.

04
Extreme Environment Construction

Undersea installations. Arctic infrastructure. Nuclear facility maintenance. Disaster response deployment. FEMA, DoD, and international relief procurement pathways.

05
Precision Industrial Assembly

Automotive, aerospace, and defense manufacturing. Large structure assembly with simultaneous long-reach and short-reach precision, a capability existing industrial robots cannot deliver.
